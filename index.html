🧶 Our Story

Born from Passion

The Desk Of Hooks began as a humble corner with yarn, hooks, and a dream — to bring the warmth of handmade things into every home.

What is Kroshay?

Kroshay (from the French "crochet") is the ancient art of creating fabric by interlocking loops of yarn using a hook. Each piece is 100% handmade — no machines, no shortcuts — just skill, patience, and creativity.

At The Desk Of Hooks, every item you see has been crafted by hand, stitch by stitch. This means no two pieces are exactly alike — you're getting something truly unique.

"Every loop is a little act of love. We pour our heart into every stitch."
